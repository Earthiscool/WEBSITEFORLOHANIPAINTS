Our Story

For decades, Lohani Paints & Hardware has stood as a vibrant pillar of the Gaya community full of color, kindness, and connection.

Founded in the Heart of Gaya

Lohani Paints began as a small family-owned shop in the early 1990s with a simple mission: to bring color and durability into the lives of the people of Gaya. Through monsoons and milestones, we stayed committed to delivering top-notch products and genuine advice to every customer who walked in.

Generations Carry the Torch

From grandfather to grandson, Lohani Paints is more than a store — it's a legacy. We’ve grown alongside the city’s neighborhoods, watched families build their dream homes, and celebrated festivals with our customers. Our commitment only deepens with each passing year.

We Thank You, Gaya

This journey wouldn’t be possible without the trust and love of the Gaya community. You’ve supported us, believed in us, and inspired us to be better every day. This isn’t just our story — it’s yours, too.

Chicky Mama - Founder

Current Shop Manager

Looking Ahead

The walls we paint today tell the stories of tomorrow. As Lohani Paints continues to grow, our heart remains rooted in the streets of Gaya, and in every smile we help color.
